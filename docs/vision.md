# Booking-Ops Vision

Booking-Ops is a creative operations platform that converts inbound booking messages into structured appointments, calendar events, and earnings insights.

## Primary user (v1)
A freelance creative (starting with models) who receives booking requests by email and needs:
- fast availability checks
- accurate calendar entries
- reliable income tracking

## Long-term goal
A universal booking + earnings layer for creatives regardless of where requests arrive (email first; later SMS/DMs/forms).

## Non-goals (v1)
- auto-replying to clients
- payments + invoicing
- multi-user teams
